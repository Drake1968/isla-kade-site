About the author

Isla Kade

Isla Kade writes near-future science fiction thrillers about institutions, secrets, and the people who risk everything to tell the truth. The Arden Vale Files is her debut series.

The series draws on the unsettling intersection of government secrecy, emerging technology, and the quiet courage it takes to ask questions that powerful systems would rather bury.
